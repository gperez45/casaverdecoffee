Coffees

Our coffees are sources from all over Latin America. Known for full roasts and tropical undertones, these blends are some of the best in the world. Ask an associate about their current favorite or for a recommendation!

Plants

We love plants! The direct translation of Casa Verde is Green House, and we live up to our name. With help from great SoCal nurseries, we are proud to bring you over 20 types of houseplants that are available for purchase.

Pastries

Our baked goods are made in house and with plenty of TLC. Featuring some Latin American favorites (think medias lunas, dulce de leche fillings, and more), the bakers are always busy!
